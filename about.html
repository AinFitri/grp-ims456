About Us

Our Delicious Journey

NAKNAK Korean Chicken is a popular Korean-inspired quick service restaurant known for its Korean Fried Chicken, premium beef burgers, loaded fries, handmade cookies, and more. The brand name "NAKNAK" signifies joy in Korean and resembles the sound of someone knocking on the door. It also means "we want" in Malay. Their goal is to bring joy through accessible and high-quality Korean food.

NakNak has been committed to perfecting the art of fried chicken. Our secret blend of spices and sauces, coupled with a dedication to quality ingredients, ensures that every piece of NakNak Chicken is a celebration of flavor and texture.
